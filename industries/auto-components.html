Auto Components

Handle complex supply chain networks

The auto components industry must support complex supply chain networks. There is a requirement to automate these networks and have a comprehensive overview of the entire value chain. Eazy ERP can help provide real-time visibility into stock movement across the countrywide value chain.

Having a better view of raw materials and finished goods inventory, relevant personnel can make improved decisions. The order entry process can be smoothened, and wastage reasons can be pinpointed and taken care of faster. Eazy ERP also allows interactive inventory management, including in-transit and physical stock.

Streamlined business processes for optimised growth

The workflows across the business must be streamlined to use optimised resources and reduce costs across the value chain. Eazy ERP has an elaborate product management module that allows you to keep track of all the components manufactured along with their specifications. Relevant personnel can make suitable changes to the specifications whenever necessary.

There is smooth data transfer among different departments to prevent complexity across the business. Eazy ERP has several in-built modules to cater to various departments across the industry, like CRM, Financial Management, Purchase, Invoice Management, Distribution Management, etc.

Comprehensive overview of the business

Eazy ERP allows you to have a 360-degree overview of the business. You can track workflows cutting across multiple departments. There is a seamless and secure flow of necessary information that authorised personnel can only access. The departments access a centralised information source and gather actionable insights through customised dashboards.

With several companies preferring the online route to sell products, Eazy ERP can completely view the customer profile. Sales managers can track performance based on customer behaviour and assess fast-moving product categories. It can help you take a customer-centric approach to your business.
