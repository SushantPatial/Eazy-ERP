Apparel & Footwear

Handling orders professionally

The business hinges on accurately determining fashion trends and faster distribution of trendy products. It is essential to have a robust system that can readily accept orders online and inform sales managers accordingly. Eazy ERP allows sales managers to receive online orders of trendy products and move the in-demand products faster.

Stock-outs must be avoided, and the stocks must be replenished at the earliest to prevent loss of revenue. Eazy ERP allows authorised personnel to create rules that will enable restocking of the in-demand products ahead of time.

Interdepartmental coordination is necessary

The apparel and footwear industry requires closed-ended workflows across multiple departments. An advanced solution like Eazy ERP can ensure tightly coupled integration for proper management and secure communication channels. Your team can leverage hassle-free communication processes a reality.

There is also the necessity to ensure secure information flow among the stakeholders. The incumbent processes may not be safe, leading to the leakage of sensitive information. Eazy ERP provides that only authorised personnel have access to sensitive information. It also assures unlimited access during office hours.

Streamline crucial company operations

The stakeholders must have a comprehensive overview of the business. A vast volume of data must be collated on a single platform. Relevant teams must store the designs they had worked on earlier and the trending and upcoming designs. Eazy ERP can help in providing secure access to such information.

Roles-based user permissions are supported in the system. It has an effective order tracking system that helps to consolidate the order to cash workflows. Data flow is secure across multiple departments in the company. The distribution process is streamlined, too, and loyalty models can be introduced faster.
