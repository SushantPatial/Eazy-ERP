Paints & Chemicals

Manage formulations and product innovation professionally

The paints and chemicals industry must handle the formulations of their manufacturing products. But several of them may have specifications that are sparingly different from each other. It requires a flexible ERP system like Eazy ERP to help them effectively store the details of the formulations of each product.

The internal teams must also ensure the raw materials are available in requisite quantities at the ideal time. They must meet the production requirements without manual intervention. Eazy ERP clients can readily track and manage the raw materials across each supplier. The suppliers can be readily informed about any changes in specifications and associated order cancellations on time.

Effective and transparent inventory management

It is necessary to have an ERP solution to modify the SKUs based on industry best practices. Eazy ERP can help the product management team adjust the SKUs according to internal and global best practices. It can also maintain the batch and lot numbers and any added description required by various clients.

It is also necessary to maintain variants of various paints and chemicals. Eazy ERP can help manage product management and easily handle rapid innovation of the products. It can help manage product lifecycles more effectively with proper collaboration among the different stakeholders across the company.

Efficient and agile supply chain

The business must have a 360-degree overview of the entire value chain. The regulatory requirements would need the company to track the finished products across the distribution chain properly. Eazy ERP can help the relevant teams to have a real-time view of the status of stocks as they move across the distribution components.

A robust ERP system like Eazy ERP can help in ensuring the suitable materials are available at the right time. It can satisfy forecast seasonal demands through historical data and send out order requests to suppliers ahead of schedule. It can also help accurate recording of inventory across various stages.
