Electrical & Electronics

Transparency in inventory handling

Sourcing the raw materials for the industry is crucial. It is essential to finalise the vendors and ensure a robust process to track the critical raw materials. The customised Purchases module allows clients better to handle contract terms with their vendors. Eazy ERP can allow processing quantities and contract terms more efficiently.

It is challenging to handle the vast volume of small electrical and electronics components across the distribution channels. Eazy ERP can devise intelligent inventory management systems to manage all SKUs. It can also help in tracking prototypes across the network.

Version control is necessary

The industry sees rapid advancements in technology. Businesses must adapt to the changing operational excellence standards to stay ahead of their peers. The products must also be updated with the latest specifications and the iterations that have happened in the past. There could be regulatory requirements requiring manufacturers to store the iterations details.

Eazy ERP can provide the platform to access historical documentation and help handle the comprehensive product lifecycle management for manufacturers. There are inbuilt modules that can be readily integrated with the product designing workflows. It can also allow automating the Bill of Materials selection to Invoicing workflow online.

Enhanced revenues through sales forecasts

Businesses must correctly leverage the vast amount of historical sales information to forecast the possible sales figures in the coming months. Eazy ERP can allow sales managers to track SKU codes and their availability against work orders. It can also enable improved visibility across the distribution channel and help sales managers overview the SKUs moving across the network.

Accurate sales forecasts can address production issues and attract higher seasonal sales volumes. The right ERP solution can also prevent the movement of faulty SKUs across the distribution channel. It is possible to create customised reports and dashboards that allow sales managers to introduce quick schemes to induce sales.
